## 1.0.0

### New Features
- **Kick Marker Picker**: A quick popup to choose which raid marker is your interrupt assignment.
- **Announce to Group**: Call out your kick marker in party or raid chat with one click, with the marker icon shown right in the message. The message text is editable.
- **Two Ready Macros**: Two draggable macro boxes in the window, one that sets focus and interrupts, and one that just sets focus and marks. Drop either straight onto your bars, no digging through the macro list.
- **Auto Macro Sync**: Both macros stay pointed at your chosen marker automatically, and they never remove or overwrite a mark already on the target when you press them again.
- **Per-Spec Interrupt**: The kick macro fills in the correct interrupt for your class and spec (Wind Shear, Counterspell, Mind Freeze, Counter Shot, Muzzle, and the rest) and updates when you change spec.
- **Macro Templates**: Each macro has its own template list in the editor. The kick macro offers the default (set focus, re-press to kick), a Ctrl-to-kick-your-target version, and an Auto Tab Kick that interrupts a casting mob without losing your current target; the set-focus macro offers target or mouseover. Hold Ctrl to kick your current target instead of your focus.
- **Ready Check Popup**: The picker opens automatically on a ready check while you are in a Mythic+ dungeon, so you can set your marker before the pull.
- **Macro Picker**: Point the addon at any of your existing macros from a scrollable list and it keeps the marker in sync.
- **Minimap Button and Options Panel**: Open everything from a minimap button or the in-game AddOns options page.
